# CeloHT No Token Policy

## Overview

CeloHT is a community-driven initiative focused on education, financial inclusion, agent networks, and environmental impact.

CeloHT does not create, issue, sell, or promote any cryptocurrency token.

## No Token Issuance

CeloHT has no native token, governance token, utility token, or investment token.

CeloHT does not plan to launch a token unless a future community decision, legal review, and transparent governance process approve such a change.

## Use of Celo Ecosystem

CeloHT uses the Celo ecosystem as technology infrastructure.

The project may use:

- CELO for blockchain transaction fees.
- cUSD for practical community transactions.
- Valora wallet for user access.

These technologies are used for accessibility, education, and real-world utility, not speculation.

## No Investment Product

CeloHT is not:

- An investment opportunity.
- A token sale platform.
- A cryptocurrency fundraising scheme.
- A financial investment service.

CeloHT participation is based on education, contribution, and social impact.

## Transparency Commitment

CeloHT commits to:

- Open documentation.
- Community governance.
- Transparent decision-making.
- Responsible technology development.

## Governance

Any future change involving digital assets must include:

1. Community discussion.
2. Legal and compliance review.
3. Transparent documentation.
4. Public communication.
