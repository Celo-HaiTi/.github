# About CeloHT

CeloHT is a community-driven Web3 initiative focused on financial inclusion, digital education, environmental sustainability, and community development.

Built on the Celo ecosystem, CeloHT creates practical solutions that connect people, businesses, and communities with accessible blockchain technology.

Our mission is not centered on speculation or financial products. Instead, CeloHT focuses on real-world adoption through education, digital payments, entrepreneurship, environmental action, and open collaboration.

CeloHT was founded with the vision of creating a more inclusive digital future for Haiti, the Caribbean, and underserved communities around the world.

# cUSD

**cUSD (Celo Dollar)** is a stable digital currency supported within the CeloHT ecosystem.

cUSD enables practical digital transactions by providing a more stable payment option for users and communities.

- Community Payments
- Merchant Transactions
- Agent Operations

- Business Payments
- Donations
- Cross-Border Transfers
