# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 20, 2026_

## Overview

CeloHT respects the privacy of its community members, contributors, partners, and website visitors.

This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, stored, and protected across CeloHT projects and services.

---

# Information We Collect

Depending on the service, CeloHT may collect:

- Name
- Email address
- GitHub username
- Wallet address (when required)
- Contact information
- Messages submitted through contact forms
- Voluntarily provided information

We do not intentionally collect unnecessary personal information.

---

# How We Use Information

Collected information may be used to:

- Respond to inquiries
- Improve documentation
- Support community programs
- Manage contributions
- Coordinate volunteers
- Publish community updates
- Improve user experience

---

# Blockchain Data

Transactions recorded on public blockchains, including the Celo network, are publicly visible by design.

CeloHT does not control or modify blockchain records.

Users are responsible for protecting their wallet credentials and private keys.

---

# Cookies

Official websites may use cookies or similar technologies to:

- Improve website functionality
- Remember user preferences
- Analyze website performance

Users may disable cookies through their browser settings.

---

# Third-Party Services

Some services may be provided through third-party platforms, including:

- GitHub
- GitHub Pages
- Vercel
- Netlify
- Google Workspace
- Celo ecosystem services

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy.

---

# Data Security

CeloHT follows reasonable security practices to protect information.

However, no internet service can guarantee absolute security.

---

# Data Sharing

CeloHT does not sell personal information.

Information may only be shared when:

- Required by law
- Necessary to provide requested services
- Authorized by the individual

---

# Children's Privacy

CeloHT does not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent where required by applicable laws.

---

# International Users

CeloHT is a global community initiative.

Information may be processed in different jurisdictions depending on the services being used.

---

# Your Rights

Depending on applicable laws, individuals may have the right to:

- Request access to their data
- Request corrections
- Request deletion where applicable
- Object to certain processing activities

Requests will be reviewed in accordance with applicable legal requirements.

---

# Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.

Material changes will be announced through official CeloHT communication channels.

---

# Contact

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy should be submitted through the official contact information provided in the repository or on the official website.
