# CeloHT FAQ

## Frequently Asked Questions

---

# What is CeloHT?

CeloHT is a community-driven Web3 initiative focused on:

- Web3 Education
- Financial Inclusion
- Agent Networks
- Environmental Sustainability
- Community Development

CeloHT uses blockchain technology to create practical solutions that benefit communities.

---

# Who founded CeloHT?

CeloHT was founded by **Johnny Dubic**.

The founder provides the initial vision and coordination, but CeloHT is governed through community participation, collaboration, and shared decision-making.

---

# Is CeloHT a cryptocurrency?

No.

CeloHT is not a cryptocurrency, blockchain, or investment platform.

It is a community initiative built using the Celo ecosystem.

---

# Does CeloHT have its own token?

No.

CeloHT does not create, issue, sell, or promote its own token.

---

# Which blockchain does CeloHT use?

CeloHT is built on the **Celo Blockchain ecosystem**.

Celo provides the infrastructure for digital payments, smart contracts, and Web3 applications.

---

# What are cUSD and CELO?

## cUSD

cUSD is a stable digital currency within the Celo ecosystem designed for practical digital payments.

## CELO

CELO is the native asset of the Celo blockchain used for network operations and ecosystem participation.

---

# What wallet does CeloHT support?

CeloHT supports wallets compatible with the Celo ecosystem.

One recommended wallet is:

- Valora Wallet

Users can use wallets to access digital payments and Web3 applications.

---

# What are the three pillars of CeloHT?

CeloHT is built around three main pillars:

## Education

Teaching communities about blockchain, digital finance, and Web3.

## Agent Network

Helping users access digital financial services through local support.

## Reforestation

Supporting environmental restoration and sustainability initiatives.
