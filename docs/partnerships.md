# CeloHT Partnerships

## Overview

Partnerships are an important part of the CeloHT ecosystem.

CeloHT collaborates with organizations, communities, developers, educators, and innovators who share our mission of expanding Web3 education, financial inclusion, and environmental sustainability.

Through partnerships, we aim to create stronger solutions and increase community impact.

---

# Partnership Vision

CeloHT believes collaboration creates greater impact than working alone.

Our partnership approach is based on:

- Shared Values
- Transparency
- Innovation
- Community Impact
- Sustainable Development
- Long-Term Collaboration
